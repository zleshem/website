My Journey

Hello! I’m Zeev (pronounced זיו in Hebrew). I’m Israeli-born, American-raised and Tel Aviv-based. I began my journey to the USA (more specifically to the great state of New Jersey) from Israel at the age of 10 and returned in 2017. My love for traveling stemmed from frequent vacations to see family and friends based in places all the way from Arizona to London to Tel Aviv.
I received my Bachelors Degree in Management in the US and began my professional career in the Aerospace industry there. After my move to the startup nation, I was intrigued to immerse myself in the high-tech industry and found myself at an internship at Airobotics Drones. And the rest is history..

6 years and 3 start-ups later, I find myself a Master’s Student at Reichman University in Human-Computer Interaction. After 6 years of professional experience under my belt, I was intrigued to challenge myself intellectually again in a subject that combines, psychology, computer science, and design, an intersection of subjects that I found myself deeply interested to learn more about.
My professional experience in the SaaS industry includes various roles that combined client-facing and technical skills including, product support, mobile app project management, customer success and solutions/sales engineering. I’m a curious problem-solver, great communicator and deeply motivated professional looking to contribute and grow alongside a promising technology company.
